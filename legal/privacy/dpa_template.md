# DATA PROCESSING ADDENDUM (Template) v1.0 (2025-11-30)
1. Parties: Controller (Organizer/Platform) and Processor (Platform/Delegates).
2. Purpose: Event/community operations, payments (Stripe), support, analytics, security.
3. Data: Account info, KYC/KYB docs, payment tokens, logs per telecom rules.
4. Processing: Only on documented instructions; APPI + applicable GDPR-like safeguards.
5. Sub-processors: Stripe, hosting, analytics; list maintained and notified.
6. Security: Access control, encryption in transit/at rest, logging, retention limits.
7. Transfers: If cross-border, ensure adequate safeguards/Standard Clauses equivalent.
8. Retention/Deletion: As per legal requirements (telecom logs) and business needs; erase/anonymize on request unless legally required to retain.
9. Audit/Notice: Breach notification without undue delay; audit rights on reasonable notice.
10. Liability: As per main agreement; no indirect damages; caps where permitted.
